## The Rally

The 2nd annual EKKO TREKK Rally was held on open BLM desert land at La Posa North, timed to coincide with the Quartzsite RV Show (January 21–29). No designated campsites, no hookups — just Ekko owners parked together in the desert within walking distance of the show, the flea market, and the gem and mineral vendors across I-10. A truly informal gathering: arrive and leave whenever you like.

## Events & Activities

- **Quigley 4x4** — vendor rep discussion
- **Winnebago** — rep discussion
- **Beer Tasting** — Friday
- **EKKO Crawl** — Saturday afternoon mod show-and-tell
- **Raffle** — Saturday evening
- **Bike Ride** and **Off-Road 4x4 & UTV Ride**
- **Yoga**, **Knitting Workshop**, **Mala Bead Workshop**
- **Meet & Greet and Bonfire** — Thursday and Friday evenings with Left-Right-Center

## The RV Show

The Quartzsite RV Show filled the weekend with hundreds of RVs to walk through, a 65,000 sq ft vendor tent, and a large flea market just north of the show with food, live music, and plenty of RV-related gear. Saturday and Sunday were wide-open days to explore at your own pace.
