## The Rally

TER 2023 was a six-day gathering at Catalina State Park, bringing together 92 Ekkos and 21 vendors for the second annual event. Highlights included a welcome party, Halloween festivities, RV mod tours, and a preview of the all-new 2024 Ekko Sprinter — shown to attendees ahead of its public release.

## Activities & Events

The schedule included morning stretching, coffee socials, group hikes and bike rides, potluck dinners, and a dedicated Alaska-bound group gathering. Mod tours gave owners a chance to explore real-world setups and swap ideas with fellow Ekko enthusiasts.
