## The Meetup

The Amana MM&MM — Mods, Maintenance & Merriment — is a volunteer-run gathering for Winnebago compact coach owners and prospective buyers. Now in its 5th year, this four-day event brings together a wonderful mix of experienced owners and newcomers to share knowledge, swap ideas, and enjoy each other's company in the heart of the Amana Colonies.

Thursday, May 14th is an optional pre-meetup arrival day; the main program runs Friday through Sunday.

## Mods & Maintenance

The centerpiece of the event is a large heated and air-conditioned garage where attendees demonstrate their RV projects — everything from electrical upgrades and solar installs to storage solutions and comfort mods. It's hands-on, informal, and one of the best places in the Ekko community to learn directly from fellow owners.

## Community & Giving Back

Group meals, a charity raffle, and a free will donation gear table round out the weekend. Extra funds raised go directly to [Shelter House of Iowa City](https://www.shelterhouseiowa.org/), a local nonprofit supporting Iowans experiencing homelessness.
