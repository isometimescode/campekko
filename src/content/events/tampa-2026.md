## The 5th Annual Tampa EKKO Rally

The Tampa EKKO Rally brought together Winnebago Ekko owners at Forgotten Angels, a private property venue in Valrico just outside Tampa. The four-day event mixed hands-on learning with plenty of social time — potluck dinners, campfires, and the kind of relaxed community feel that makes these rallies worth the drive.

## Talks & Seminars

The schedule was packed with practical sessions across two days:

- **Lithionics** — battery system overview
- **Electrical Systems** — presented by FreedomVanGo
- **Latch Lock Tutorial** — FreedomVanGo
- **Tire and Weight Safety** — presented by RVSEF
- **Jack Points & Spare Tire** — hands-on demonstration
- **Truma Talk** — heating and hot water systems
- **Water Sanitizing & Generator Oil Change**
- **La Mesa RV** — vendor presentation
- **Skymed** — travel protection overview
- **Alaska Discussion** — group planning session
- **Q&A with Winnebago Team**

## Social Events

Friday's Winnebago-sponsored dinner was followed by a Q&A with the Winnebago team. Saturday brought a novelty dog show, group photo, and raffle before a catered dinner sponsored by Skymed and La Mesa RV, capped off with trivia night. Evenings ended around the campfire.

## Giving Back

Raffle and 50/50 proceeds benefited [Forgotten Angels](https://forgottenangelsflorida.org/), the host venue and nonprofit organization.
