## The Rally

TER 2024 was a five-day gathering of Winnebago Ekko owners at Catalina State Park — the third consecutive year at this beloved venue. With 95 Ekkos and 19 vendors in attendance, the event was the community's biggest yet before TER 2025 topped it. As a thank-you to the park that hosts us every year, the rally donated $4,000 to the [Friends of Catalina State Park](https://friendscsp.org/).

## Schedule Highlights

Each day opened with guided hiking and biking options across the park's trails. Saturday featured guided mod tours where attendees could walk through fellow owners' rigs, followed by a Music in the Mountains concert and a Beer & Brats gathering with a prize drawing. Sunday's Chili & Cornbread Potluck was a crowd favorite. Monday wrapped up with a stargazing session led by Ranger Isaiah Ortiz, and a farewell dinner.

## Talks & Presentations

- **Owl Vans** — John Willenborg walked through products designed specifically for the Ekko
- **Winterizing your EKKO** — Tom DePottey covered storage and maintenance procedures
- **The EKKO Remote Office** — Daniel Azuma and Ada Pang shared their work-from-van setup
- **Extended Boondocking** — Bob Simmons on strategies for two-week off-grid trips
- **FreedomVanGo** — Grant Wilson presented products and answered Ekko-specific questions
