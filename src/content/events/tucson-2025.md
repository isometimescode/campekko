## Event Overview

TER 2025 was a volunteer-driven gathering of Winnebago Ekko owners held at Catalina State Park for the fourth consecutive year. This event centered around connection, learning, and hands-on experiences and was the biggest event yet! Attendees traveled from across the US and Canada to camp together, share ideas, and immerse themselves in all things Ekko.

## Learning, Mods & Vendor Support

A big focus of the rally was improving and personalizing rigs. Vendors and experts were on-site offering live demonstrations, advice, and even free installations for pre-ordered upgrades, giving attendees a rare chance to enhance their vehicles with professional help.
Mod tours and technical sessions allowed participants to explore real-world setups, learn about gear, and gain confidence in maintaining or upgrading their own Ekkos.

## Activities & Outdoor Experience

The rally blended structured sessions with plenty of time to enjoy the surrounding desert. Group hikes, biking, and informal meetups encouraged people to explore Tucson’s natural landscape together. More than anything, this rally was about connection. Dozens of rigs and travelers came together, quickly forming a tight-knit community through shared meals, campfire conversations, and social activities. The event fostered friendships, knowledge-sharing, and a sense of belonging that extended beyond the rally itself—turning a simple meetup into a memorable, community-focused experience.
