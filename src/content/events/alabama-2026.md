A Campout is a great way to make more friends and learn about your EKKO. We will have a potluck, play games and have great campfire conversations.

# Registration
There is no additional fee other than your campground reservation. We have a reserved section (F) at the campground, so when you make your reservation, please state that you are with EKKO Campout.
