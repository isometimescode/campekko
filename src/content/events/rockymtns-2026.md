## Please join your EKKO community at the 2nd Annual Camp EKKO! 🎉

This year is a big change from 2025 — we’re holding it at a beautiful full-hookups RV park just 30 minutes from Rocky Mountain National Park in Northern Colorado. The resort has a paddling lake, a rainbow trout fishing lake, bike rentals, two restaurant/bars, pickleball, bocce ball, volleyball, basketball, a swimming pool, a gym, golf cart rentals, and more.

No registration fees — you only pay for your campsite directly to the park.

## HOW TO REGISTER

⚠️ IMPORTANT BEFORE YOU BOOK: Do not book online and do not call the national reservations line (so, do not press 1 when you call the #). Both will put you outside our reserved block at a higher price. Follow the steps below to get a Camp EKKO site.
1. Reserve your campsite:
Call Sun Outdoors Rocky Mountains directly at *970-557-0200*, then press 4, then press 5, and you may have to leave a voicemail — they’re great at calling back. When they call you back, tell them you’re reserving for Camp EKKO, 3 nights, August 27–30. They’ll ask you to choose your site, so have the site map handy (posted below). Sites range from $72–$106/night.
2. [Fill out the registration form (after you have your site number)](https://forms.gle/b26MFAkMvC8R52aH6). This gets you on our list for email updates as plans come together.
3. Hit “Going” on the Facebook Event
    This will (hopefully) get you Facebook notifications when we post updates here in the event. That said, Facebook is unreliable about this — so check back here occasionally and look under the Posts to stay in the loop.

VENDORS: If you offer products or services specific to EKKOs, we’d love to have you! Email stef@thefitrv.com for details—we have a dedicated section for you. In exchange for a spot, we ask that you host informal mini-chats/demos/activities at your campsite throughout Friday and Saturday — repeated a couple of times each day to keep crowds small and the vibe relaxed.

Hope to see you in Granby! 🏕️​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​
