## 🌵 Spring Rally in the Desert

Join fellow Winnebago and camper van enthusiasts for a fun-filled Spring Rally in the Las Vegas desert from April 14–19, 2026. Designed especially for Ekko owners (and beyond), the event blends community, learning, and adventure with vendors, seminars, Q&A sessions, and group activities.

## 🚴 Activities & Highlights

Enjoy a wide mix of experiences, including scenic tours, hands-on classes, and vendor showcases. A major highlight is the Tour de Summerlin bike ride alongside Red Rock Canyon, with options for all skill levels. Expect mod tours, swap meets, and plenty of opportunities to explore new gear and ideas.

## 🎉 Daily Fun & Community

Each day offers something different—from casual arrival hangouts and excursions to seminars and vendor days. Evenings feature social events like bowling night and a Saturday fire pit send-off, followed by a lively Latin dance party to wrap up the rally.

## 🏕️ Camping & Extras

The rally is hosted at a well-equipped KOA near Sam’s Town, offering full hookups, amenities, and group gathering spaces. After the event, an optional caravan heads to Valley of Fire with continued camping and potential extensions to Death Valley or Arizona.
